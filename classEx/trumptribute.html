The Life of Steven Paul Jobs

Steven Paul Jobs was born on February 24th, 1955
Born and raised in Mountain View, California
Attended Homestead High School from 1968 to 1972
Attended Reed College, 1972
Drops out of Reed College, 1972
Begins working for Atari inc. in 1974
Wozniak and Jobs create Apple, 1976
Resigns from Apple in 1985
Creates "NeXT" inc. in 1985
Returns to Apple in 1997
Resigns from Apple in 2011 due to health issues
Steven Paul Jobs dies on October 5th, 2011
